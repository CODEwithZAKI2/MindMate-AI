# MindMate AI – Product Specification

## App Goals
- Provide accessible, private mental wellness support through AI-powered conversations
- Help users track and understand their emotional patterns over time
- Deliver personalized, non-clinical wellness exercises
- Ensure user safety with proactive crisis detection and resource provision
- Build trust through transparent privacy practices

## Target Users
- **Primary:** Adults (18-45) experiencing everyday stress, anxiety, or low mood
- **Secondary:** Individuals seeking self-improvement and emotional intelligence tools
- **Excluded:** Users requiring clinical mental health treatment (app will recommend professional help)

## MVP Features
- User authentication (email, Google, Apple Sign-In)
- AI wellness chat with context memory
- Daily mood check-in (5-point scale + optional notes)
- Basic mood history view (7-day trend)
- Safety detection with crisis resource display
- Disclaimer acceptance on first launch

## Future Features (Post-MVP)
- Premium subscription tier
- Advanced mood analytics (30/90-day trends, triggers correlation)
- Guided breathing exercises with animations
- Journaling with AI-generated prompts
- Push notification reminders
- Export personal data
- Multi-language support

## Non-Goals (What the App Must NOT Do)
- ❌ Provide medical diagnosis or treatment recommendations
- ❌ Prescribe or suggest medications
- ❌ Replace professional therapy or counseling
- ❌ Store or process data for third-party advertising
- ❌ Allow AI to respond to active crisis situations (must show resources instead)
- ❌ Call Gemini API directly from the mobile app
