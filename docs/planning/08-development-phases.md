# MindMate AI – Development Phases

## Phase 1: MVP (Weeks 1-8)

**Goal:** Launch a functional app with core wellness features

| Week | Focus | Deliverables |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 1-2 | **Foundation** | Project setup, CI/CD, Firebase config, auth flows |
| 3-4 | **Chat Core** | Chat UI, Cloud Function, Gemini integration, safety filter |
| 5-6 | **Mood System** | Mood check-in, history view, basic charts |
| 7 | **Polish** | Onboarding, disclaimer, error handling, offline support |
| 8 | **Testing & Launch** | QA, TestFlight/Internal Testing, bug fixes, store submission |

**MVP Feature Set:**
- ✅ Email + Google Sign-In
- ✅ AI wellness chat (10 messages/session context)
- ✅ Daily mood check-in
- ✅ 7-day mood history
- ✅ Crisis detection + resources
- ✅ Basic settings

## Phase 2: Enhancements (Weeks 9-16)

**Goal:** Improve engagement and add premium value

| Week | Focus | Deliverables |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 9-10 | **Analytics** | 30-day trends, mood patterns, weekly insights |
| 11-12 | **Exercises** | Breathing exercises, journaling prompts |
| 13-14 | **Engagement** | Push notifications, streaks, achievements |
| 15-16 | **Premium** | Subscription system, premium features gate |

**Phase 2 Feature Set:**
- ✅ Advanced mood analytics
- ✅ Guided breathing with animations
- ✅ Journaling with AI prompts
- ✅ Push notification reminders
- ✅ Streak tracking
- ✅ Premium subscription (RevenueCat integration)

## Phase 3: Scaling & Monetization (Weeks 17-24)

**Goal:** Scale infrastructure and optimize business model

| Week | Focus | Deliverables |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 17-18 | **Localization** | Multi-language support (Spanish, French, German) |
| 19-20 | **Performance** | Caching, CDN, cold start optimization |
| 21-22 | **Features** | Export data, family sharing, dark mode polish |
| 23-24 | **Growth** | Analytics integration, A/B testing, referral system |

**Phase 3 Feature Set:**
- ✅ Multi-language (5 languages)
- ✅ Performance optimizations
- ✅ Data export (GDPR)
- ✅ Family plan (share subscription)
- ✅ Referral rewards
- ✅ Advanced analytics (Mixpanel/Amplitude)
